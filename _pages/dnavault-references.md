[PubMed 21310274](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/21310274/)

**GERMLINE: Population-scale hidden relatedness**

[PubMed 18971310](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/18971310/)

**ERSA: Maximum-likelihood shared ancestry estimation**
